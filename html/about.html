Hi, I'm Tekendra 👋

I'm a Computer Engineering student at Far-Western University, Nepal, with a deep passion for cybersecurity, Linux systems, networking, and web development. I believe in learning by doing every project, CTF challenge, and open-source contribution teaches me something new.

My journey into tech started with curiosity about how systems work under the hood. That curiosity turned into a love for Linux, which led me down the rabbit hole of networking and, eventually, cybersecurity. Today I focus on understanding how systems can be compromised so they can be better defended.

Beyond the keyboard, I co-founded the Cyber Security Club at Far-Western University (CSC-FWU), where I work to build a community of security-minded engineers. I enjoy sharing what I learn through blog posts, workshop talks, and open-source projects.

My long-term goal is to work as a security engineer or penetration tester contributing to making the internet safer for everyone. I'm always open to collaboration, mentorship, and new challenges.
